SHOP DATABASE MANAGEMENT

  • Category: Program
  • Programming Language: C
  • Topic: Data Structures (Linked List), File Handling

Detailed Problem statement

The problem statement is to store the data of the customers and their cart till they leave the shop. And also print the bill for the customer when he makes the final payment.

Functionalities/features supported by the project

The problem statement is to store the data of the customers and their cart till they leave the shop. And also print the bill for the customer when he makes the final payment.

Description about selected data structure and reason to select it

  • It can store any number of items in the shop, provided the name and price of the items are saved in the ‘item.txt’ file.
  • The attributes of the item are ID, name, price.
  • The ID of each item are generated by itself in order of their insertion.
  • It can store any number of customers.
  • The attributes of customer are ID, name, and cart (to store the items that they have selected to buy)
  • The user can switch customer any time they want.
  • The data of the customer are stored in the program till the make the final payment of their. After that the data is deleted.
  • Customer can add or remove items from their cart any number of time till they make their final payment.
  • When a customer makes the final payment , the bill is generated in the ‘bill.txt’ file.

Any special algorithm or method used for implementation

We have taken input from the file and make a linked list out of it for the list of items of the store. Also the bill generated is put onto a file so that we could print it..
